Purpose, Goals and Audience:

The purpose of the website is to serve as a platform for the fast food chain - O’Burger, which will both inform and intrigue our audience about the products, prices, services and other information through it’s appealing and well-structured design, giving them the best possible browsing experience. The site is aimed at anyone who loves a good burger, but especially younger people and shtudents, who are given special discounts. It will also be aimed towards people who want to reach out to the brand, such as business partners.

Navigation Structure:

Our navigation structure will make it possible to access any of our sites regardless of the sub-page that you’re on. The structure gives the user the ability to navigate through all of the pages in one click, making it easy for anyone to browse through our pages. You will be able to navigate through these pages with one click: ‘Home-page’, ‘Menu’, ‘Prices & Ordering’, ‘Deals’, ‘Find restaurant’ and ‘About O’Burger’ (see site map below). We chose this method because it is the easiest and least time consuming way of finding the information that you need on our website.

Content:

  1. Homepage - index.html

    The homepage will be the first page that the users will see when they visit the site. Here we’ll have a navigation bar where you can navigate to different parts of the website with one click. The homepage will also include a picture slideshow. At the bottom of the site there will be contact information on customer service and also a short FAQ. The main content of the homepage will be our deals and student discounts. These will be displayed as a slideshow, which is going to capture the user’s attention as soon as he/she enters the page. The slideshow is going to run automatically, but the user can also click through the different deals. We took our mobile users into consideration, and decided to not make their slideshow automatic.

  2. About Us - about.html

    This page will contain information about the history of O’Burger and the food.

  3. Menu & Deals - menu.html

    This page will contain the restaurants full menu, which will contain burgers, salads, desserts, drinks etc. Each food will have one picture and it will be easy for the user to see the price.

  4. Order - order.html

    This will be a form website where the user will choose what they want from the menu and then fill out information for the delivery. We will use javascript to give the user feedback about the order.

  5. Find Restaurant - find_restaurant.html

    This page will make it possible to find our restaurants based on the city that you choose in a select-menu. The restaurants in the chosen city will show up on the page with the use of javascript, and display the address and contact information of the given O’Burger restaurants in the city.

  6. Contact Us - contact.html

    This page will contain a form where you can enter your e-mail address and send a mail directly through our form on the page. The e-mail will reach the customer support. The customer support’s phone number will also be displayed on the page.
